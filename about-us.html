Building Tomorrow’s Enterprise 

Technosys is one of the leading Structural steel design & detailing company offering services to the structural steel industry since year 2014. Technosys enables clients in more than 3 countries to outperform the competition and stay ahead of the innovation curve. With 40+ employees, we provide our clients cutting edge solutions by providing optimized solutions in the field of design & Fabrication.

The Technosys Story  

In 2014, 2 entrepreneurs started Technosys. From the beginning, the company was founded on the principle of building and implementing great ideas that drive progress for clients and enhance lives through enterprise solutions. For over eight years, we have been a company focused on bringing to life great ideas and enterprise solutions that drive progress for our clients.

We recognize the importance of nurturing relationships that reflect our culture of unwavering ethics and mutual respect. It’ll come as no surprise, then, that 97 percent (as of March 31, 2021) of our revenues come from existing clients. Technosys has a growing global presence with clienteles across India & Middle East.

At Technosys, we believe our responsibilities extend beyond business. That is why we established the Tunga Foundation – to aid some of the more socially and economically depressed sectors of the communities in which we work. And that is why we behave ethically and honestly in all our interactions – with our clients, our partners, and our employees.
